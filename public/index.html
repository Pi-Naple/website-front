How it works

Gain insights on your clients and generate passive revenues

The Pinaple allows you to do so much more than just sell your WiFi. You can analyse your clients browsing habits, get detailed insights on your revenues and get business related advice.

1

Easy setup

Our solution is the simplest on the market : plug the Pinaple and you are ready to go.

2

Connect up to 120 devices

Our device allows you to connect plenty of clients to one hotspot. If you need more, we handle that too.

3

Connect multiple Pinaples together

Need to connect more client ? No problem, plug another Pinaple and your ready to go !

4

Protect your clients' data

All the traffic on the Pinaple goes through Mullvad's VPN. Your clients are protected and free to browse the web. Enjoy our automatic ad filtering as well.

We anonymise and study your clients data to provide you with the best information about who your are.

IA powered business tips

Our data allows us to give you hacks for your business. Your clients really like beer ? We'll make sure to let you know so you don't miss opportunities.
